RegExp Cheatsheet

Anchors
^ start, $ end, \b word boundary, \B non-boundary
Common
. any, | or, (...) capture, (?:...) non-capture, (?=...) lookahead, (?!...) neg. lookahead
Classes
\d digit, \D non-digit, \w word, \W non-word, \s space, \S non-space
Quantifiers
* 0+, + 1+, ? 0/1, {n}, {n,}, {n,m} (append ? for lazy)
Email
^[^\s@]+@[^\s@]+\.[^\s@]+$
URL
^https?:\/\/[\w.-]+(?:\.[\w\.-]+)+[\w\-\._~:\/?#\[\]@!$&'()*+,;=.]+$
IPv4
^(25[0-5]|2[0-4]\d|1?\d?\d)(\.(25[0-5]|2[0-4]\d|1?\d?\d)){3}$
Hex color
^#(?:[0-9a-fA-F]{3}){1,2}$
ISO Date
^\d{4}-\d{2}-\d{2}(?:[T\s]\d{2}:\d{2}(?::\d{2})?Z?)?$
Slug
^[a-z0-9]+(?:-[a-z0-9]+)*$
UUID
^[0-9a-fA-F]{8}-[0-9a-fA-F]{4}-[1-5][0-9a-fA-F]{3}-[89abAB][0-9a-fA-F]{3}-[0-9a-fA-F]{12}$
Password (8+)
^(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z])(?=.*\d).{8,}$
